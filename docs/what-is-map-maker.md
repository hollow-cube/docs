# What is Map Maker?
Map Maker is a place where you can create Minecraft maps of three archetypes: Building, Parkour, and Adventure. 
It takes the core ideas of those three map types and expands upon them, giving creators many useful tools to work with.

We offer a platform where people can share their work, get feedback, improve by seeing others' work, and find other 
users to connect with and play or build things together.

You will see a vast range of content, from outright beginners to professionals in the industry, all categorized and 
rated for you to easily sort through and find whatever you want. However, some people may not publish their work, instead
choosing to keep it private to store their creations, practice specific parkour jumps, build with a team for another
project, etc. 

There is no true end goal or objective. People can climb leaderboards or aim for the best stats, but the main 
gimmick is just to have fun, and since most content is community-driven, there is an unlimited amount of it!
