# Savestates

## Explanation

For every map that you play, you will have a savestate. This includes your position, stats, time spent, and any other
relevant information for the map, such as potion effects. No matter the reason you have to leave a map, you needn't 
worry, because you can just come back later and continue your progress right from where you left off!
:::note Note:
Position saving is extremely precise, and includes your X, Y, and Z coordinates, as well as your facings (Pitch and Yaw). 
:::

## Completing a Map
Upon finishing a map, you will receive a diamond helmet and be able to fly around. Furthermore, your now completed savestate 
will be recorded and categorized, and you will be able to see it, as well as compare it to your best run (shortest completion 
time). Rejoining the same map after this will start a new savestate for you.

## Spectator Mode
Sometimes you'll want to do things in a course that are outside the normal confines of play. Spectator mode allows you to
do this with ease. You can toggle it on or off via the middle item in your playing hotbar, or with `/spec`. Once activated, 
all progress on your map will be paused. You will then be given a golden helmet and the ability to fly. Additionally, you 
can toggle your flight status on or off, teleport to other players to watch them play, and set artificial checkpoints to 
practice parkour with before a real attempt.

## Resetting Your Progress
If at any point you want to restart a map, you must double-click the reset item in your playing hotbar, or quickly type 
`/reset` twice in a row. Alternatively, if you have not reached a checkpoint yet, using the return to checkpoint item will 
bring you back to the start of the course. If you would like to play a map from its beginning without resetting your current 
progress, you're out of luck. However, we will, in the future, have an update that allows players to create and choose from 
multiple savestates when playing a map. There is no timeline for this, but know that it IS planned.
:::warning WARNING:
You CANNOT undo a reset of your savestate. Once it has been deleted it is gone for good.
:::
